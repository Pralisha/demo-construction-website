Affordable Prefab Homes

Get the same luxury as a site-built home, but at a fraction of the cost when you opt for a prefabricated home. Unlike traditional homes, prefab homes—a.k.a. packaged homes—are built in sections at a building facility and transported to your home site where it's assembled. Because of this, you save money on labor and sourcing materials without sacrificing on quality.
